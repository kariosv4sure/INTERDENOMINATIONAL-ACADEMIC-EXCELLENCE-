Our Journey to Excellence

INTERDENOMINATIONAL ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE [I.A.E]™ was born from a simple but powerful vision: to provide every Nigerian student with access to quality education and admission guidance, regardless of their background or location.

Founded by Mr. Daniel Moses, a passionate educator with over 12 years of experience in academic coaching, I.A.E has grown from a small tutorial center into a comprehensive educational ecosystem serving thousands of students across Nigeria.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized mentorship. We don't just teach; we walk with our students every step of the way — from exam preparation to university admission and beyond.
