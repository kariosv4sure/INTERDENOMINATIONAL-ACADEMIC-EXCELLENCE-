Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JAMB and our services

How do I get my JAMB profile code?
Send your NIN to 55019 via SMS (e.g., NIN 12345678901). We can help you complete this process and purchase your e-PIN.
What's the difference between UTME and Direct Entry?
UTME is for students with O'LEVEL results seeking 100-level admission. Direct Entry is for those with A'LEVEL, Diploma, or NCE qualifications seeking 200-level admission.
How can I change my course or institution after registration?
You can apply for a change of course/institution through JAMB portal. We assist with the process and ensure all requirements are met.
What is CAPS and how do I check my admission status?
CAPS is JAMB's Central Admission Processing System. You can check your admission status by logging into your JAMB profile. We help monitor and resolve any CAPS issues.
